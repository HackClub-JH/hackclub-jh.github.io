Code of Conduct

The required standards of conduct for the Hack Club community & events.

Overall

  • Treat everyone with respect and kindness.
  • Think carefully about the way you communicate.
  • Don't be toxic or inconvenient.
  • Don't use spamming or aggressive words.
  • If you encounter a problem, send an email to hackclub.jh@gmail.com .

Why do we have a Code of Conduct?

The Hack Club community has people with many different perspectives. Hack Club employees are responsible for providing a friendly, safe and welcoming environment for everyone, regardless of personal characteristics such as age, disabilities, gender, nationality, race, religion, sexuality, or similar.

Moderation and Punishments

Please understand that what you do and say has consequences, and unwanted behavior will not be tolerated. By participating in the communities where the Code of Conduct applies, you must act in accordance with our values. If you act in a manner that is explicitly prohibited by the Code of Conduct, you will be alerted and asked to stop, and your posts may be removed by community moderators. In case of recidivism, you can be banned temporarily or permanently
